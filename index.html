The Continuous Project

Rather than saying we speicalize in one thing we tend to do it all, so we like to call ourselves a project but more so a group of creatives trying ever so hard to help elivate musicians careers however we can. Starting out as an idea based in a college dorm room in 2018, Spruce St. Projects has turned into a nationally recognized marketing outlet to whom Founder and CEO, Brett Iannucci, has worked on projects associated with artists Korn, AFI, Smashing Pumpkins, Third Eye Blind, System Of Down and more. Our company specializes in the development of an artists career from all corners of the industry, we got you covered. With a thorough background in artist development, distribution, marketing, listener growth and more our team is determined to get you heard anywhere and everywhere! So enough about us, we want to hear from you. drop us a line below and let's create.
