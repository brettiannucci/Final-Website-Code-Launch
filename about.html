The Continuous Project

Created originally as “Nooch Entertainment” in the winter of 2018 our company began as an artist management firm based out of Lancaster City, Pennsylvania. From there on our founder and CEO, Brett Iannucci, was attending Millersville University working with the university on a number of different projects relating towards the booking of large scale bands such as Third Eye Blind, Young The Giant and more as well as managing several clients musical needs in the nearby area. Once realizing that his education wants and needs were to change with an interest in musical marketing he transferred to the nationally recognized music department of the University Of The Arts located in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. After spending several semesters studying under their industry professional staff as well as achieving a number of different scholarships and internships he decided to relaunch his company as a marketing and management preferred company with the key emphasis on it being a “project”. Since then he has been involved in a number of different projects involving groups such as AFI, Smashing Pumpkins, System Of A Down, Korn, North Kingsley as well as a number of different associated artists.
